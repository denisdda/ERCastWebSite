The members of the team come from the Technical Circle established at the "Elie Radu" Energetic Technological High School about five years ago. There have been over 100 students in this circle over the years. The ERcast team has 15 members and 4 mentors. The robotics circle is aimed at high school students aged 14 to 18 who are passionate about STEM (science, technology, engineering and mathematics) subjects. Students and mentors work in teams to create a robot, organizing themselves in design, assembly, configuration and programming activities (including webinars - online courses).
The name of the ERCast team was obtained as a result of the acronym of the high school (Liceul Technologic Energetic "Elie Radu"). The values of the team are reflected in its name: Education, Respect, Communication, Action, Success and Strength of Character. Together, they form the principles of the ERcast family:
"The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams!" (Eleanor Roosevelt).
We chose this motto for the FTC contest because it encourages us to set our ideals and guides us towards achieving our dreams.
